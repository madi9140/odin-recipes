Chocolate Chip Cookies

This is a recipe for tasty chocolate chip cookies! This is my actual recipe so hope you like it!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract (I use Trader Joe's bourbon vanilla gel)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup chopped semisweet baking chocolate

Steps

  1. Preheat overn to 350 Degrees
  2. Mix butter and sugars until smoothly combined
  3. Stir in egg and vanilla until uniform
  4. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt
  5. Continue mixing until a soft, tacky dough
  6. Mix in chopped baking chocolate
  7. Portion into 12 equal balls and place 2 inches apart on the baking sheet
  8. Bake for 10-13 minutes
  9. Let cool 20-30 minutes to set and enjoy
