Loaded Potatoes

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious loaded baked potato!

Ingredients

  • 1 Russet Potato
  • 1 Cup Cheese
  • 3 Tbs Chopped Bacon Bits
  • 3 Tbs Sour Cream
  • 3 Tbs Butter

Steps

  1. Bake the potato
  2. Let the potato cool
  3. Add butter, sour cream, and bacon bits
  4. Enjoy!
